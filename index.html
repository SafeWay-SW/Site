About-us

Adverse and unexpected situations end up to deconstructing old habits, changing the patterns and lifestyles of everyone who is somehow affected by them. The COVID 19 pandemic has been demonstrating very strongly the need for a balance between health, safety, hygiene and economy. In this sense, we created SAFE WAY, a mobile web platform that will bring security to the consumer and reduce risks. Our mission is to enable the implementation of a high level of hygiene standards for micro, small and large companies, and also individual enterprise, self-employed and local producers. We will bring safety for you Family meals.
