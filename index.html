About-us

Adverse and unexpected situations end up to deconstructing old habits, changing the patterns and lifestyles of everyone who is somehow affected by them. The COVID 19 pandemic has been demonstrating very strongly the need for a balance between health, safety, hygiene and economy. In this sense, we created SAFE WAY, a mobile web platform that will bring security to the consumer and reduce risks. Our mission is to enable the implementation of a high level of hygiene standards for micro, small and large companies, and also individual enterprise, self-employed and local producers. We will bring safety for you Family meals.

We help you choose 100% sanitized food.

We want to see you away from COVID-19.

We care for your health.

WHAT THE NEW WORLD HAS SHOWN US?

The Earth images allowed us to observe the considerable reduction in the emission of polluting gases, mainly by vehicles, such as SO2 and NO2, which indicates a great reduction of human traffic on the streets, showing a greater permanence of people in their homes and, therefore, greater use of delivery services. In this sense, our goal is to make this activity feasible in a pandemic context, in order to adapt them to the proper hygiene standards and at the same time reassure the consumer when contracting the service! That’s SAFE WAY's proposal for our community!
